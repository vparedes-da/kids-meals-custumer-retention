# kids-meals-custumer-retention


The Industry was investigated, to know what are the challenges that this kind of industry faces in general. It was found that in a big picture this is a Food & Beverages industry, but also is consider e-commerce, DTC ( direct to customer), and suscription-based.

A companie with this caracteristics usually faces challenges in customer retention, logisticts due to the DTC model, the diferents regions where the delivery price change, demand due to seasons but also the kid's needs and tastes.

For this case This analysis is focused on Customer retention - Pricing & Profitability and the question that will be anwsered is : How to reduce the churn in a suscription-based ? To answer this question will be necessary to respond sub questions such as : is the churn product related? How long have the people on the suscription be before they cancel? base on that are the cancelations from short term suscriptions? etc.

So the phases for this projectes are :

1. Cleaning data
2. Analysis
3. Model
4. Recommendations
